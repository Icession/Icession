I'm a full-stack and mobile developer based in Cebu, Philippines, currently completing a BS in Information Technology at Cebu Institute of Technology – University (graduating 2027).

I design, build, and ship applications end to end — from UI/UX in Figma through frontend, backend, database, and deployment. I work AI-first, using Claude and Gemini as part of my daily development workflow to ship faster while keeping ownership of every decision and line of code.
